Currently Supporting MHA

We're currently supporting MHA (Mental Health America). We believe that during this quarantine, many are struggling with their mental health and need support. Many people have to go through the loss of others, the loss of their jobs, and the lack of socialization with others. We believe that Mental Health America is an organization combatting this problem. To donate to MHA, click the donate button below. If you'd like to see more information about them, visit the MHA website. We will be hosting an event to support MHA in the future, so look forward to that!

Also Supporting CHOC

We're also supporting CHOC (Children's Hospital of Orange County), which was our main focus in the last event we hosted (eSports Tournament). You can help the children in need by donating a few dollars through this link. Visit the CHOC website for more in-depth information.
