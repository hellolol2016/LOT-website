WHY SCHOOL SUPPLIES?

15 million students in the US lack the money to purchase basic school supplies.

Students that have their own school supplies are more excited about their learning, have improved grades, and better attendance.

Over 90% of teachers have to spend money buying supplies for their students. On average, teachers spend nearly $500 a year in out of pocket expenses for classroom supplies.
