eSports Tournament

Say hello to our first ever fundraising project! We are going to be hosting an eSports tournament in the first week of September. Although we will charge participants an entry fee, which will all go towards CHOC. Participants willing to enter can directly donate to CHOC as the entry fee, which we will check before allowing them in. We, as a very present organization, firmly believe that supporting healthcare providers during these tough times is very necessary, and what better way to do so through an activity which high schoolers enjoy as well? The games that are going to be played in the tournament are Valorant, CS:GO, Overwatch, and Chess. The prize pool is $195, and you can have a chance (impossible) to win all of that. But, at the end of the day, it's about having fun and helping people in need in whatever way you can at the same time!

Feel free to contact us, we would love to hear from you!
