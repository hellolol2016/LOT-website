eSports Tournament

Sign up ended 9/13/2020.
Say hello to our first ever fundraising project! We are going to be hosting an eSports tournament during the September 19-20 weekend. Interested participants do not have to pay to enter this event, however, donations will be HIGHLY encouraged and appreciated. We as a present organization, firmly believe that supporting healthcare providers during these tough times is very necessary, and what better way to do so through an activity which high schoolers enjoy as well! Check out our donate page if you are interested.

The games that are going to be played in the tournament are Valorant and Chess. The prize pool is $75, so look forward to some highlights going up on our YouTube channel!

Feel free to contact us, we would love to hear from you!
