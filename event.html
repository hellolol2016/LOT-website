The Mental LOTline

Did you know that 80% of your thoughts in a day are negative? It might be the time for you to take a break and listen to that little guy inside your head. From simple headaches to severe depression, there’s so much that you are yet to know about your mental health. Each month, our hosts will be joined by an expert in the mental health field to tackle different aspects of mental health, so sit back, relax, and let your mind drift as you join our journey to #breakthestigma.
