eSports Tournament

Say hello to our first ever fundraising project! We are going to be hosting an eSports tournament in the first week of September. Participants willing to enter can directly donate to CHOC as the entry fee, and we'll check for proof before letting them in. Proof can come in the form of a screenshot or receipt, but must show who donated. We, as a very present organization, firmly believe that supporting healthcare providers during these tough times is very necessary, and what better way to do so through an activity which high schoolers enjoy as well? The games that are going to be played in the tournament are VALORANT, CS:GO, Overwatch, and Chess. The prize pool is $195, and you can have a chance (basically impossible) to win all of that. You can refer to the flyer below to see the prize for each game. But, at the end of the day, it's about having fun and helping people in need in whatever way you can!

Feel free to contact us, we would love to hear from you!
