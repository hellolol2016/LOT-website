COVID RESPONSE

In the current state of the world, we're unable to host any physical events. As a result, we have to resort to online events. However, this doesn't stop us from coming together as a community and helping others that need our assists. Through online events and advertisements, we are committed to support organizations that are doing good work, especially our medical community). As a community, we can show our biggest gratitude by taking care of each other and spreading awareness of their good work.

Here are some things you can do:

Support our hospitals

We're currently supporting CHOC (Children's Hospital of Orange County), so you can help the children in need by donating a few dollars by clicking the donate button below. More in depth information on the Donate Page and the CHOC website.

Pick up a passion

During this quarantine, everybody has so much free time due to the fact many of activities involve going outside. Without these activities you may pick up new passions that do not involve going outside such as cooking, drawing, or graphic design. Exploring new passions during this time is a very good way to spend your pastimes since its productive, and can be beneficial to you.
