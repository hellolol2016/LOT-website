Our Mission

We utilize YOUR skills to benefit the community

Our mission is to empower and inspire the community to do good by utilizing the skills of compassionate students. Here at Laugh Out Together Foundation, we believe every member of our community is unique in their own way, and we strive to bring out the best of our community. As our name suggests, we envision a world where everyone can showcase the best of themselves, and our community can truly "laugh out together".

Student Run

We're a foundation based around Orange County and completely run by students. We're currently working around Irvine in multiple high schools such as Beckman, Woodbridge, Northwood, and University. We are here to prove that our age and status will not limit our eagerness to unify the community. Often, students provide refreshing views and unique opportunities for an issue. Through a student-led leadership, we strive to connect passionate students with those in need of their help and showcase the talents of the youth community through team-based projects.

Our Vision

As our name suggested, we envision a world where everyone can showcase the best of themselves, and our community can truly “laugh out together”. Today, we live in an age where incentive and peer-pressure dominates our passion. In the midst of stereotypes and prejudice, the society seems to be setting a definition for what is useful and who can be useful. With a set definition, many around the community are left behind.

Laugh Out Together foundation is here to change this definition. We believe that everyone, no matter their age and status, has an unique passion that can impact the community. Through unconventional volunteer projects, we hope to create a platform that showcases the unique skills and talents of our community. With the platform we create, we also hope to raise resources to empower individuals in combating their physical and mental challenges.

A little from you, a lot for the world! Together, we will make a difference.
