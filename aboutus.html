Our Mission

Through utilizing the skills and hobbies of our compassionate youth community, LOT Foundation seeks to provide resources that empower individuals in combating their physical and mental challenges.

We're a foundation based around Orange County and completely run by students. We're currently working around Irvine in multiple high schools such as Beckman, Woodbridge, Northwood, and University, with the purpose of contributing money and human services to helpful organizations and companies that need it, while employing the talents and interests of our members. Our foundation is here to prove that our age does not determine our usefulness to our community.
