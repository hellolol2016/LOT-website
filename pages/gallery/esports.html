Esport Tournament

We’re not going to lie, many of us at LOT are gamers. In fact, many games hold a close place to our hearts. In order to show our love for video games, have some fun, and publicize ourselves as well, we’ve made it our objective to hold an annual LOT eSports Tournament and inviting teams from all over to compete.

We recently held our second annual tournament, and we’ve had so much fun all around playing Valorant, League of Legends, and chess! Look out for the signup for next year's!

Feel free to contact us, we'd love to hear from you!
