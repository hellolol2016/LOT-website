What have we done?

The LOT Foundation was founded in the summer of 2020, and we've been growing ever since. It was a dream to bring together youth from Orange County so they can use their talents for a just cause. We empasize on the unique qualities of every single member because we believe everyone has the potential to make a difference.

Hover over each ofthe icons below to see an important step in LOT's history!

Laugh Out Together Foundation was born. Amid a global pandemic, we set to forge a movement that will individualize volunteerism and inspire the community to do good. (June 2020)

Our first member’s meeting! Despite having to conduct the meeting online, Twenty-one members showed up with hopes to utilize their talents to do good for the community.

Our first ever fundraising project was an eSports tournament held on September 19-20. Two games were played, namely Chess and VALORANT. We were supporting the Children's Hospital of Orange County during this event, and we raised over $500 from humble participants, donors, and members of the organization.

The first LOT Chapter is established by a group of 6 students at Beckman High School with a starting membership of 25 members. (October 21, 2020)
