Donate

We're currently supporting MHA (Mental Health America). We believe that during this quarantine, many are struggling with their mental health and need support. Many people have to go through the loss of loved ones, the loss of their jobs, and the lack of socialization with others. COVID also creates a very different and hard environment for those who attend school or work, but we believe that Mental Health America can help combat this problem. More in depth information on the Donate Page and the MHA website.
